How detection works

One clone leaves a dozen fingerprints. We read all of them.

Omega Guard never relies on a single tell. It continuously compares a deep set of metadata signals to confirm whether a site is impersonating your brand.

SIG_01

Favicon Hash

Catches the copied browser-tab icon that scammers almost always forget to change.

SIG_02

Structural Design

Compares the underlying layout and code structure of the suspect site to yours.

SIG_03

Visual Fuzzy Hashing

Scores how closely the graphics and brand representation match your real assets.

SIG_04

Domain Age

Days-old domains posing as established businesses are an immediate red flag.

SIG_05

Domain Control Records

Examines ownership and control records that legitimate operators don't hide.

SIG_06

Hosting ASN Reputation

Weighs the reputation of the network hosting the suspected clone.

📡

Early-warning sensors attach to your sites and report anyone deviating from normal traffic patterns while probing your pages and profiles — flagging the reconnaissance that comes before a clone goes live.
