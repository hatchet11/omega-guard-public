What members get

Verify the real business. Find and shut down the fakes.

Two jobs, one membership: prove your business is authentic to the customers about to pay you, and continuously hunt the look-alike sites built to impersonate you.

Verified Business registry

We confirm your authentic domains, phone numbers, and approved payment channels, then issue a public trust mark any consumer can check before they pay.

Domain-control verification

A DNS-TXT challenge proves a member actually controls each domain before it is ever marked verified. No self-asserted claims.

Clone & impostor monitoring

Continuous sweeps across DNS permutations, certificate-transparency logs, passive DNS, and brand search surface the look-alikes the moment they appear.

AI-assisted triage

Every candidate is scored and reviewed. The AI looks at the site artifact, never at people, and a trained human confirms before anything is acted on. AI involvement is always disclosed.

Evidence & chain of custody

DOM, WHOIS, certificate, and archive snapshots are captured and SHA-256 hashed, with a full custody log, so a confirmed impostor stands up to a takedown or a referral.

Alerts & takedown packets

When an impostor is confirmed, the member is alerted and handed a ready-to-file takedown packet with the evidence already assembled.
